Welcome to CollegeHockeyTransfers!

Have you found yourself picking up your phone multiple times per day to check social media, news sites like CHN, USCHO, etc., your favorite team's homepage, and more? Do you wish you could get notified as soon as a player associated with your favorite team(s) commits, transfers, or goes pro? If yes, then you're in luck! CollegeHockeyTransfers is a free service that will automatically send you an email each time a player transfers to or from a team you've signed up for updates from.
