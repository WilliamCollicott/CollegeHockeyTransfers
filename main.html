Welcome to CollegeHockeyTransfers!

Have you found yourself picking up your phone multiple times per day to check social media, news sites like CHN, USCHO, etc., your favorite team's homepage, and more? Do you wish you could get notified as soon as a player associated with your favorite team(s) commits, transfers, or goes pro? If yes, then you're in luck! CollegeHockeyTransfers is a free service that will automatically send you an email each time a player transfers to or from a team you've signed up for updates from. Please look below and select the team(s) you'd like to be notified about, enter your email, and then click "Submit".

How do I sign up?
To sign up, scroll down and click on the button(s) belonging to the team(s) whose transfers you'd like to be notified about. If you'd like to be notified about any transfer corresponding to a team in a certain conference, you can click the conference's button in the "Sign up by conference" section instead of havint to click on each team's individual box. To de-select a conference or team, simply click the button again. Once you've selected the conference(s) or team(s), scroll to the bottom of the page and enter your the email address that you'd like the email notifications to be sent to. Finally, click "Submit".

How does this service work?
At a regular interval, several times per day, a Python script will execute that checks the EliteProspects.com transfer feed for transfers corresponding with NCAA D1 hockey teams. If it sees any, it will send out an automated email to those who have requested to hear about transfers involving that particular team.

What kinds of transactions will trigger an email to be sent?
For example, let's say you've signed up to get notified about Michigan Tech. You will receive an automated email to the address you entered in the following scenarios: A player commits to playing at Michigan Tech in a future season, a player transfers to Michigan Tech from a different college team, or when a current Michigan Tech player transfers to a different team (either college or pro).

What will the automated email look like?
For example, if the team is Michigan Tech, the email's subject line will be: [CollegeHockeyTransfers] Michigan Tech Transfer Alert

The email's contents will look something like this:
John Doe (F) from Michigan Tech to Detroit Red Wings
Status: Confirmed Transaction
Date: 01/01/2024
Information: 3 years

The email will also include a link to the player's EliteProspects profile page as well as a picture of the player, if their profile page contains one. The "Information" line will only appear if the transaction report on EliteProspects included those additional details. The "Date" indicates the date on which the transaction will take place, so it's possible it will be in the future. Furthermore, the "Status" can be a rumor as well.

How do I unsubscribe or change the team(s) whose transfers I want to receive emails about?
To make a change to the team(s) you previously submitted, please send an email to william@collicott.com from the email address you previously registered/submitted and explain what change's you'd like to make, or if you'd like to unsubscribe from ALL emails.
